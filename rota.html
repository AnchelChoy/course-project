Welcome to Rota

Rota is the southernmost island in the Northern Mariana Islands, known for its unspoiled natural beauty, friendly communities, and rich cultural heritage. It offers a peaceful retreat with stunning landscapes and a slower pace of life, perfect for travelers looking to experience authentic island life.

Historical Sites and Diving

Must-Visit Historical Sites in Rota

  • Chugai'Pictograph: Explore ancient rock art created by the indigenous Chamorro people, showcasing their rich cultural heritage.
  • Latte stone Quarry: Visit the site where the iconic latte stones, used as pillars for traditional Chamorro houses, were quarried.
  • Tonga Cave: Discover this natural cave system that holds historical significance and offers a glimpse into Rota's past.


Top Diving Spots in Rota
